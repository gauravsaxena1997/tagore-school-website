ADMISSION PROCEDURE

Admission details for session 2018-19

Tagore public high sr. sec. school ,jaipur welcomes you to class i admission process for the acedmic session 2018-19.
Eligible Age: Born between 01.10.2011 and 31.03.2013 (Both the dates included) i.e. Minimum age 5 years to 6.5 years as on April 01, 2018.

1
Registration forms will be available online, only through our website.
2
You will receive an E-mail and SMS after successful submission of registration form.
3
False information / incomplete forms liable to rejection. No request for correction will be entertained.

Guidelines filling the online registration form

1
Read all the instructions and click on the button given at the bottom of this page.
2
Registration Form shall appear, read it and fill it carefully and properly. (Incomplete forms will not be accepted by the software).
3
The Date of birth of the child should be entered exactly as per the information mentioned in the birth certificate.
4
It is mandatory to enter all the fields which are marked in red colour.
5
Email address and Mobile Phone No. of the parents is must and it will be used by school for future correspondence.

Documents to bring to school at the time of admission (Deposition of school fee)

1. Two recent passport size photograph of the child.
2. Self attested copies of birth certificate (issued by government authority) and Adhar Card of candidate (Along with original for verification)
3. Self attested copies of Pan Card / Adhar Card of both the parents (Along with original for verification)
4. Proof of Residence
5. Proof of Annual Income
6. Photocopy of Mark sheet of class last attended
7. Certificate of minority (if applicable)
8. Certificate of caste (if applicable)
